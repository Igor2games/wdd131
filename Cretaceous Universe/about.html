About Fossil United

Fossil United is dedicated to merging the thrill of scientific discovery with unforgettable prehistoric adventure. Our mission is to inspire curiosity about Earth’s past and make the dream of seeing real dinosaurs a reality!

How We Brought Dinosaurs Back

Through groundbreaking developments in genetic engineering, our team recovered and reconstructed viable dinosaur DNA from ancient mosquito fossils. With passion, expertise, and a relentless drive for safe innovation, Fossil United’s paleobiologists achieved the impossible: the first living dinosaurs in 65 million years. Cretaceous Universe is the world’s only park where science meets adventure. Walk among giants, safely and responsibly.
